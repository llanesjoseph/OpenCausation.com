Structured Medical Causation Review

OpenCausation.com is being developed as an introductory gateway for understanding structured medical causation review.

The site will provide general information about how diagnosis–exposure questions are evaluated, including consideration of:

  • Timing
  • Mechanism
  • Exposure details
  • Objective findings
  • Individual health factors
  • Competing explanations
  • Documentation quality

OpenCausation.com is currently under development. Additional information and resources will be available soon.
